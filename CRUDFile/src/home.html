Who We Are

NYS Cosmetics is a leading platform for makeup enthusiasts in Malaysia. Our website serves as a comprehensive resource for individuals looking to discover the latest makeup trends, beauty tips, and product reviews. We aim to empower our users with the knowledge they need to make informed decisions about their makeup choices.

Our Mission

At NYS Cosmetics, our mission is to simplify the makeup research process for Malaysian consumers. We strive to curate a diverse range of product information, including detailed descriptions, user reviews, and expert recommendations. Our goal is to empower individuals to make confident and informed purchasing decisions, ensuring a seamless and enjoyable makeup shopping experience.

Our Team

Behind NYS Cosmetics is a dedicated team of makeup enthusiasts and industry professionals. Our diverse team is passionate about providing valuable insights and guidance to the makeup community in Malaysia. We work tirelessly to ensure that our platform remains up-to-date with the latest trends, product releases, and beauty tips, catering to the diverse needs and preferences of our users.
